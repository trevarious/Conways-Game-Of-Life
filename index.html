Conway's Game Of Life

Rules Of The Game

Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies, as if by underpopulation, any live cell with two or three live neighbors lives on to the next generation, and any live cell with more than three live neighbors dies, as if by overpopulation, while any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes a live cell, as if by reproduction
